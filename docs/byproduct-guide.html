🪨 Byproduct Optimization Guide

How to maximize value from stone byproducts and dust processing.

⚠️ Critical Correction: The Ore Smelter produces 1 stone per 2 ores (not 1 per 1). The Blast Furnace produces 1 stone per ore but has a worse bar multiplier (×0.9 vs ×1.2). This affects all stone-dependent calculations.

Use Ore Smelter for better bars. The Blast Furnace's extra stones rarely compensate for the 25% bar value loss.

Stone Priority Order

Process stones in this exact order for maximum value:

1. 🔍 Prospect through all 5 prospectors — EV $141.03/stone

2. 🧱 Pair surviving stones for concrete bricks — $78/stone

3. 🔥 Odd stone → fired brick or glass — $48/stone (brick) or $30/stone (glass)

4. 💎 Process any gems found — Polish → Cut → use in products

💡 Never sell raw stones! Even the simplest processing (crush → brick → kiln) turns a near-worthless stone into a $30 kilned brick.

Sifting: Bonus Ore Recovery

Regular Sifter

$4,000 — 10% chance to convert dust into a random ore

Dust is not destroyed on failure. The 90% that fail continue as dust for brick/glass processing.

Nano Sifter Prestige

⭐ 1 Medal — 16.6% chance, rarer ore distribution

Direct upgrade. Higher success rate and better chance of valuable ore types.

📊 Sifting placement: Place the sifter after the crusher but before any dust processing (brick mold, kiln, clay mixer). Dust that fails the sift continues through the line normally.

Complete Stone Workflow

Optimal Flow for Every Stone

Step 1: Stone → Prospectors (all 5) → 22.62% become gems → process gems (polish → cut)

Step 2: Surviving stones (77.38%) → pair up for concrete bricks

Step 3: Unpaired odd stone → crush → brick mold → kiln → polish → QA ($48)

Step 4: If you need glass/circuits: divert some dust to kiln for glass ($30)

Per-Ore Stone Economics

Since the Ore Smelter produces 1 stone per 2 ores, the per-ore contribution from stone byproducts is:

Gem EV per ore: $141.03 × 0.5 = $70.52

Concrete brick per ore: $78 × 0.5 × 0.7738 = $30.17 (only survivors)

Total byproduct EV per ore: ≈ $100.69

This is in addition to bar value from the main smelting line.
