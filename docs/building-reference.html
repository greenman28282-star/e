🏗️ Complete Building Reference

All buildings sorted by price. Prestige machines and transport listed separately at the end.

💡 Tip: Prices shown are wiki-verified. Focus on Core machines first, then expand into Combination and End-Game tiers as income grows.

Upgrade Machines

NamePriceEffectDescription
Ore Cleaner$80+$10 to uncleaned oresFirst upgrade you should buy. Cleans ores before smelting to increase bar value.
