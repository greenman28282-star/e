🗺️ Stage-by-Stage Factory Layouts

Each stage shows the optimal factory layout at that investment level. Prestige machines appear automatically when toggled above. Values update live.

Stage 1 — Starter Factory

~$710 investment

Machines: Ore Cleaner ($80) · Polisher ($250) · Ore Smelter ($380)

Ore Line ⛏️ Mine→ ⭐ Ore Upgrader→ 🧹 Ore Cleaner→ ✨ Polisher→ 🔥 Ore Smelter→ 💰 Sell Bars
Duplicate 📋 Duplicator → Copy bar at 50% value before selling
Stones Smelter outputs 1 stone per 2 ores → sell or stockpile for Stage 2

Per-Ore Value Breakdown

Ore → Clean (+$10) → Polish (+$10) → Smelt (×1.2) = Bar worth $36.00

Stones: Not processing yet — low value. Focus on bars.

Total per ore: $36.00 + $18.00 duplicate

💡 Priority: This is your starter layout. Save up for Stage 2 machines quickly — stone processing is where major income starts.

Concrete Survivors→ 🔀 L/R Split→ 💥 Crush (half)+ 🪨 Stone (half)→ 🧪 Cement Mixer→ 🧱 Brick Mold→ 🔥 Kiln→ ✨ Polish
Duplicate 📋 Duplicator → Best on casings, rings, or circuits

🔀 Layout tip: The L/R Splitter on the stone line creates a perfect 50/50 split for concrete brick production: one stone crushed to dust, one stone kept whole for the Cement Mixer.
